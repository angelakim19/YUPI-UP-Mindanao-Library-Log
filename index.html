Welcome to YUPI

Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the visitor management system at UP Mindanao Library by providing a seamless, efficient, and secure platform for logging visits. We aim to enhance the library experience for students, professors, and visitors through innovative technology that ensures accurate record-keeping, improved resource management, and heightened security.

Vision

Our vision is to become the benchmark for digital visitor management systems in academic libraries, fostering an environment where technology and tradition merge to create a vibrant, efficient, and user-friendly library experience. We aspire to support the academic and cultural pursuits of the UP Mindanao community by ensuring our library remains a welcoming, accessible, and well-managed resource center.

Goal

Our goal is to implement a state-of-the-art visitor log website that: a.) Provides a user-friendly interface for seamless log-in and log-out; b.) Tracks detailed records of library visits, including areas visited and times; c.) Enhances security and resource management within the library; d.) Offers valuable data insights for optimizing library operations; e.) Encourages continuous improvement in library services and user satisfaction.
